IELTS MODULES

IELTS evaluates your English language skills in 4 modules (Reading, Writing, Listening and Speaking).

LISTENING - 30 MINUTES

You listen to the recordings and answer them according to your understanding and interpretations.

READING - 60 MINUTES

Consisting of 40 questions, this section is designed to test a wide range of reading skills.

WRITING - 60 MINUTES

Task will be in two segments where one will be comprehensive and other will be essay writing.

SPEAKING - 11 TO 14 MINUTES

Test to assess your spoken English and is conducted in three segments.
